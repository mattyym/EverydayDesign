At Everyday Design, we combine timeless style with everyday comfort so your spaces look beautiful and work beautifully for real life.

Our Story

Founded in South Lyon, Michigan, Everyday Design grew from a love of transforming simple rooms into meaningful spaces. We approach each project with curiosity, care, and a discerning eye—whether it’s a single-room refresh or a full-home transformation.

We listen closely, design with intention, and guide you through decisions so the process feels clear and enjoyable from start to finish.

What We Value

  • Listening first. Your needs, routines, and tastes shape the design.
  • Beauty + function. Spaces should be stunning—and truly livable.
  • Attention to detail. Materials, lighting, scale, and flow matter.
  • Clarity. A guided process that keeps choices simple and stress-free.
